ABOUT US


From Italy to the US

Dolce Bacio concept starts to form in Italy, where founders Dante and Francesca begin to toying with the idea of bringing the authentic and sophisticated flavors of Italy outside the borders.
In 2015, with a pastry degree in one hand and a dream in the other, they leave for Sydney Australia, where they open the first Dolce Bacio food truck.
After 3 years of Australian life, it is time to sail to America, where Dolce Bacio finds its new home in a beautiful emerging city, Austin TX.
Since then, a lot has changed.

Dolce Bacio delivers a superior tasting experience, bringing you a touch of real Italian atmosphere, enhanced by our all Italian staff and high quality artisan Gelato.
